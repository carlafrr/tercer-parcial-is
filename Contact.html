+

Absolutely! I have experience working with clients from all around the world. Through effective communication channels such as email, video calls, and project management tools, I ensure seamless collaboration regardless of geographical location.

How long does it typically take to complete a web design project?

+

The timeline for a web design project varies depending on its complexity and scope. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a more complex project with custom features could take 8-12 weeks. I'll provide you with a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes! I offer ongoing website maintenance services including regular updates, security monitoring, content updates, and technical support. I can create a custom maintenance package tailored to your specific needs and budget.

Absolutely! I implement SEO best practices in every website I build, including proper meta tags, semantic HTML structure, optimized images, fast loading times, and mobile responsiveness. I can also provide ongoing SEO consulting services.

Yes! I have experience integrating various third-party tools and platforms including payment gateways, CRM systems, email marketing tools, analytics platforms, and social media feeds. Just let me know what integrations you need.
